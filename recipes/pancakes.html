Fluffy Pancake Recipe!


Description

Need a fluffy pancake recipe? This one is just right, especially when there's plenty of butter and syrup. Make it extra special with berries and cream!

Below are the ingredients and the instructions you will need for this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup of Milk

  • 2 TablesSpoons of white vinegar

  • 1 cup of All purpose flour

  • 2 Tablespoons of white sugar

  • 1 Teaspoon of baking powder

  • 1/2 Teaspoon of baking soda

  • 1/2 Teaspoon of salt

  • 1 Egg

  • 2 Tablespoons of butter, melted

  • Cooking spray

Instructions

  • Step 1

    • Gather all Ingredients from the list.

  • Step 2

    • Combine the milk and vinegar in a medium sized bowl and set aside for
    5 minutes to make sour milk.

  • Step 3

    • Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large
    mixing bowl.

  • Step 4

    • Whisk egg and butter into soured milk.

  • Step 5

    • pour flour mixture into milk mixture and whisk until lumps are gone.

  • Step 6

    • Heat a large skillet over medium heat and coat it with cooking spray. Pour 1/4
    cupfulls of batter onto the skillet, and cook until bubbles form and the edges are
    dry, about 2 to 3 minutes.

  • Step 7

    • Flip with a spatula and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat with
    remaining batter. Serve with some syrup or what ever sauce you desire!
